Design Criteria Analysis
Brand

Sophisticated- A simple white based background. Minimal color except in the ads and the features. The audience is not the average magazine reader. Flaboyant images do no dominate the fold. The distinct style of this webpage is defined by the typography. Notice the "W" in The New Yorker. Although there is a cartoon style, the cartoons revolves around political/artisitc/etc figures rather than the chitter-chatter of popular tabloid rags.

Politically Charged. Articles surrounding the US election, Thatcher, outsourcing are featured articles. Examines the issues in congress and internationally.

American. The dominant colors are white, red, and blue. The heading "The New Yorker" calls attention to the notion that New York is America.
