Design Criteria Analysis
Brand ...
Functionality The site is a creative designers portfolio. There is a colorful tagline and a simple explanation of what the designer does. He listed his skills, a link to his portfolio, and his contact information in an artistic fashion all on the front page - giving the viewer easy access to any information they would need.
Interactivity Most of the links on the page react when you hover over them. Links change color and tabs give off the effect of being pressed. He also allows you too smoothly shift between Introduction, Web Design, and Printing & Branding by the tabs at the bottom or the arrows.
Navigation The site uses a simple navigation bar to get to the four main pages of the site. The link that is active is a different color so you know where you are, and still know where you can go. The home page gives more links - which was confusing at first, but it turns out that they all lead to the same places you can get with the navigation bar. It is confusing because it makes the site seem larger at first glance that it really is.
